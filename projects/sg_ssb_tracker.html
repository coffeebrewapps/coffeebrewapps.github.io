Singapore Savings Bonds Tracker

An app to track SSB allotment and monitor interests payment.

This app allows users to track their SSB allotments since the first issuance in October 2015, and monitor the interests issuance by month for the upcoming 10 years. A simple dashboard displays at a glance this year’s total interests and the breakdown of the calculation.

Built with:

vuejs
vuetifyjs
